# Product Vision Document Template

Based on the One-Flow Upload Vision ({{COMPANY}} {{SELLER_TEAM}}, 2025) — the internal benchmark for vision document structure and quality.

---

## Usage Notes

- Fill every section from available inputs. Mark genuinely unknown fields as `[TBD — research needed]`, not placeholder text.
- Sections marked *(optional)* can be omitted if not relevant to the scope.
- The vision statement is the single most important output. Do not rush it.
- All data claims must cite a source or be marked `[estimated]`.

## Quality Checklist Before Finalizing

- [ ] Vision statement passes the "four attributes" check: lofty, realistic, devoid of today's limitations, grounded in a potent problem
- [ ] Vision statement is vivid and specific — you can close your eyes and picture it
- [ ] Problem Statement includes at least one hard data point
- [ ] Every phase has a North Star metric, at least one Secondary, and one Guardrail
- [ ] No section is left with generic placeholder text — either filled or marked `[TBD]`
- [ ] Executive Summary can stand alone and tell the whole story in 30 seconds
- [ ] Guiding Principles are actionable (a team member could use them to make a decision)
