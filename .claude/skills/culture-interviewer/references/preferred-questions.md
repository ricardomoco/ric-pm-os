# Preferred Culture Interview Questions

These are the interviewer's preferred questions. Attributes are mapped to the official {{COMPANY}} culture scorecard.

1.  **Why {{COMPANY}}?**
    *   *Attribute:* Engagement with our purpose & mission

2.  **Have you ever used {{COMPANY}}? What did you think of the experience? Likes/Dislikes**
    *   *Attribute:* Engagement with our purpose & mission

3.  **What are 3 things you hope to find at {{COMPANY}}? And 3 you wish not to?**
    *   *Attributes:* Inclusion, Curiosity, Empathy, Integrity, Collaboration, Openness

4.  **Tell me about a time when you received tough feedback. What was your reaction? What actions did you take? What did you learn from this?**
    *   *Attributes:* Empathy, Inclusion, Openness

5.  **Can you share an instance where you learned something valuable from a colleague? Or something you learned that helped your team?**
    *   *Attributes:* Curiosity, Openness

6.  **Tell me about a frustrating situation. What happened, what would you have done differently?**
    *   *Attribute:* Growth Mindset

7.  **Describe a situation where you had to learn a new skill or tackle a project outside of your comfort zone. How did you approach it, and what strategies or resources did you use to achieve success?**
    *   *Attribute:* Growth Mindset

8.  **How do you incorporate users' feedback and data into your work?**
    *   *Attribute:* User Centricity

9.  **Give an example of how you simplified a process to reduce user friction.**
    *   *Attribute:* User Centricity
