# {{COMPANY}} Culture Attribute Definitions

To meet the bar, a candidate must provide **specific, relevant, and tangible examples** of their experiences. Generalities, vague answers, or purely aspirational statements ("I always try to...") must be treated as **[Claims]**, not **[Evidence]**.

## Attributes & Definitions

*   **Curiosity:** A proactive desire to explore, learn, and understand new ideas, technologies, or perspectives beyond one's immediate scope.
*   **Empathy:** The ability to seek out and truly understand the feelings, perspectives, or motivations of others, especially in collaborative or conflicting situations.
*   **Integrity:** Acting with honesty and adhering to strong moral principles and ownership, even when no one is watching or when it is personally disadvantageous.
*   **Openness:** Embracing transparency and a genuine willingness to listen to others, share information freely, and adapt one's own viewpoint when presented with better data.
*   **Inclusion:** Actively valuing, respecting, and seeking out the unique contributions and diverse backgrounds of all team members.
*   **Engagement with our purpose & mission:** A clear, demonstrable connection to {{COMPANY}} core values of conscious consumption, the secondhand market, and sustainability.
*   **Growth Mindset:** The belief that abilities can be developed through dedication and hard work; a focus on learning from failure and frustration.
*   **User Centricity:** A deep commitment to identifying and solving for the actual needs and frictions of the end-user.
*   **Collaboration:** Working effectively across boundaries to achieve shared goals, prioritizing the team's success over individual ego.
